# Cold Outreach to Fellow CMs

Paige sends these 1:1. LinkedIn or text if she has the number. Never mass. Never scheduled. If it reads like a template, delete.

**Target list:** CMs / superintendents / PMs she has personally met or crossed paths with. Residential preferred. 10–30 sends total in launch week.

---

## Template 1 — Someone she's worked with before

**Subject (if email):** Something I built for closeout

**Body:**

Hey [name] — Paige. Hope [specific project or mutual contact] is going well.

I've been building a punch-list tool on the side for the last few months because I got fed up with retyping everything at 10pm. Finally shipped it this week.

If you're doing closeout on a legal pad or a shared spreadsheet, I'd love a second pair of eyes on it. Founding-user trial, free for 7 days, no card. I'd rather you try it than pitch you.

Link: buildcoreoperations.com/#founding

If it's useful, great. If it's not, I still owe you a beer.

— Paige

## What NOT to do

- Don't send to anyone you haven't actually interacted with
- Don't mass-merge. Each send has at least one specific reference to that person
- Don't follow up more than once. If they don't reply, move on
- Don't pitch in the first message beyond the single product line
- Don't attach a PDF, deck, or sales sheet. One link. One line. That's it
- Don't use the phrase "quick question" — it's cold-email poison
