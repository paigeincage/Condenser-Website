The old way:

- Walk the house with a legal pad

- Split it by trade (drywall / paint / plumbing / trim)

20+ hours a week. Just for closeout.

**3/**

"Nick on drywall, master bath, paint."

"Drain runs slow, hall bath, plumbing."

Hit send. Every sub gets their list in the channel they actually read (text for the plumber, email for the cabinet guy).
