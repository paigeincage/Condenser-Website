I built a tool.

Not because I thought I could beat Procore. Not because I wanted to start a company. Because I was tired of retyping punch lists at 10pm after my kids went to bed.

If you've never worked in residential closeout: walk the house, find every defect, photograph it, categorize by trade, type it up, split it into separate emails, send to each sub. Repeat 4–6 times a week. Twenty hours a week of desk work that happens after the "real" day is done.

So I built The Condenser.

You dictate while you walk. It groups items by trade automatically. You hit send — every sub gets their list in the format they want. Texts the plumber. Emails the cabinet guy. Logs it. Done.

What used to take me 90 minutes takes 15.

Today we're opening 50 founding-user spots. If you're a CM, super, or PM in residential — especially if you're still doing closeout on a legal pad — come try it. Free for 7 days, no card. You'll get founding pricing locked in and, honestly, you'll shape what we build next.

No venture capital. No growth team. One CM, one dev, one tool, trying to make closeout less soul-crushing.

Link in comments.

— Paige
