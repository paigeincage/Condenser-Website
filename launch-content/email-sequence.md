**Subject-line tone:** no "🔥" emojis, no "You won't believe." Short, human, field-voiced.

## Email 2 — Day 3 (subject: "Did your first walkthrough work?")

**Send:** 72 hours after signup

---

Hey — Paige here.

Quick check: have you done a walkthrough yet?

If yes: what broke? I need to know. Reply with a sentence — that's all.

If no: what's blocking you? I bet I can unblock it in 60 seconds. Reply and tell me.

The two things founding users usually get stuck on:

- **"It didn't hear me"** — speech capture needs HTTPS and mic permission. If you're on an older Android in dusty conditions it sometimes bails. Text input works the same way, no pride lost.
- **"I can't find my contacts"** — import from a vCard or CSV in Settings → Contacts. Takes 30 seconds.

I'm building this with you, not for you. So actually tell me what's wrong.

— Paige

## Technical notes

- All sends from `hello@buildcore.dev` (set up in Beehiiv → publication settings)
- Replies route to Paige's personal inbox via Beehiiv forwarding
- UTM: `?utm_source=beehiiv&utm_campaign=founding-user&utm_content={email_name}` on every link
- Plain-text variants (Beehiiv handles automatically)
- No images in the first email — improves deliverability on day 1
- A/B test subject lines on email 2 and 3 once we have 100+ subscribers
