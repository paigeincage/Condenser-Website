## Tagline (60 characters)

Built for the field, not the office.

## Short description (260 characters)

The Condenser is a punch-list tool for residential CMs. Voice capture in the field, trade-grouped auto-dispatch, offline-first so it works on lots with zero signal. Built by a construction manager who got tired of retyping at 10pm.

## Gallery captions

1. **Walkthrough view** — Tap or talk. Items auto-group by trade as you add them.
2. **Send modal** — One tap, every sub gets their list in the channel they read. Text, email, or copy.
3. **Offline-first** — Your phone is the source of truth. Sync happens when signal comes back.
4. **Contact routing** — Import your trade contacts once. Condenser knows who gets what.
5. **Homeowner updates** — Photo + one paragraph, branded, sent. Inbound calls drop.

I'm a construction manager in Austin. I close out 3–6 houses a week. For two years my closeout workflow was:
