Core Pipeline

Five phases. Zero guesswork.

Every punch list follows the same disciplined path — from raw intake to confirmed resolution. No items fall through the cracks.

📥
Phase 01
Intake
Upload PDFs, snap photos, capture voice notes, or type manually. Every source gets labeled and tracked.
🧪
Phase 02
Condense
AI extracts individual items, classifies by trade, and auto-assigns to the right partner using your field language.
📋
Phase 03
Organize
Master list grouped by trade. Filter by source, status, or priority. Hot items and aging flags surface what matters.
📨
Phase 04
Communicate
Send items grouped by trade partner via email, text, or clipboard. Customizable templates with your sign-off.
🔍
Phase 05
Follow-Up
Re-walk the house, update statuses, add new items. The list is always current, the record always complete.
Capabilities

Every tool a CM needs.
Nothing they don't.

Purpose-built for residential construction managers who manage 5–20 active lots and need speed over features.

🏗
Trade Auto-Classification
Field language aliases, keyword mapping, and community defaults auto-route every item to the right trade category.
🔥
Priority & Aging System
Mark items HOT for immediate attention. Items untouched for 72+ hours get flagged as stale. Nothing hides.
🎙
Voice Capture
Speak your punch items while walking the house. Voice is transcribed, split into individual items, and classified.
📂
File Management
Organize by project with five folder categories: Inspection Reports, Photos, Plans, Permits, and Correspondence.
📧
Template Messaging
Customizable email and text templates with dynamic variables. Send complete trade-grouped lists in one tap.
📒
Trade Reference Directory
Searchable card-based directory with contacts, companies, and processing managers. CSV import supported.
🚶
Follow-Up Walk Mode
Pick a project, re-walk the house with date and walker metadata. Update statuses and add items in real-time.
📱
Mobile-First PWA
Designed for one-handed iPhone use in the field. Works offline, installs like a native app, survives bad signal.
📤
Share & Export
Copy, save to file, text, email, print, or use native share. Get the list out however your partners need it.
